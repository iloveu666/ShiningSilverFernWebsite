    FAQ

    Do you bring your own supplies and equipment?

    Yes — we bring eco-safe agents, cloths and a commercial vacuum. If you prefer specific products, tell us during booking.

    How are carpet and window prices calculated?

    Carpet is per bedroom; windows are per window with a bulk cap — the estimator shows both and picks the better value automatically.

    Do you service my area?

    Greater Auckland (CBD, West, North Shore, East and South). For sites outside these areas, contact us for availability.
