FAQ

Do you bring your own supplies and equipment?

Yes — we bring eco-friendly cleaning agents, cloths and a commercial vacuum. If you prefer specific products, just let us know.

How does linen service work?

We can collect, wash, dry and return linen, or use a laundry partner. For Airbnb changeovers we can also provide hotel-grade linen by request.

Which areas do you cover?

Greater Auckland including CBD, West, North Shore, East and South. For sites outside these areas, contact us for availability.
